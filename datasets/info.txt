1. The dataset inside "pfd-ds-icst" is distributed in compressed directories according to aircraft flight states.
2. The dataset inside "pfd-ds" is the PFD simulation data in the form of images.
3. The dataset inside "pfd-sim-ds" is simulator raw data for the PFD simulation.
4. The dataset inside "gcs-cds-ds" is the GCS-CDS simulation data in the form of images.
5. The dataset inside "gcs-cds-sim-ds" is simulator raw data for the GCS-CDS simulation.
